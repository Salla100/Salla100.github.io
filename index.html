// about

The person
behind the PCB

I'm a dual Norwegian–German engineer finishing my integrated Master's in Electronic System Design and Innovation at NTNU Trondheim, specialising in Space Systems. I thrive at the intersection of hardware precision and systems thinking — I can design a PCB and reason about the whole satellite architecture.

From interning at ESA ESOC to leading electronics at ORBIT NTNU and building satellite payloads at TU Berlin, I've been hands-on with every phase: concept, CDR, assembly, integration, and ops.

When I'm not soldering or simulating, I'm playing saxophone (16+ years), exploring the outdoors, or falling into a good Sci-Fi novel.
